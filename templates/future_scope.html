Future Scope of ChefBot

ChefBot is an AI-powered recipe recommendation system integrated with pantry management. In the future, the system can be enhanced with intelligent automation and improved personalization to provide a smarter cooking experience.

Enhanced AI Recommendation System

Improving machine learning models to provide more accurate and personalized recipe suggestions based on user behavior and ingredient history.

Image-Based Ingredient Recognition

Integrating computer vision to allow users to upload images of ingredients and automatically detect items for recipe suggestions.

Nutrition & Calorie Analysis

Providing nutritional information and calorie tracking for recommended recipes to support healthy eating habits.

Smart Cooking History Tracking

Maintaining user cooking history to recommend recipes based on previous preferences and frequently used ingredients.

Expiry & Low Stock Alerts

Implementing automatic notifications for low-stock or expiring ingredients to reduce food wastage.
